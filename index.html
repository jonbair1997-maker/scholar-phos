About

Scholar Phos is a study Bible app focused on close reading. It bundles three public-domain English translations and adds a Greek New Testament interlinear with Strong's lexicon, public-domain commentaries, cross-references, parallel translations, and personal notes, highlights, and bookmarks.

It runs on iPadOS 26 and macOS 26 and later.

Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-07-03

What Scholar Phos collects

Nothing. The developer does not collect, store, sell, or share any personal information. The app contains no analytics, no advertising, no tracking, and no third-party SDKs, and it requires no account or sign-up to use.

Your study content

Notes, highlights, bookmarks, workspaces, sermon drafts, and Apple Pencil annotations you create are stored on your device. If you are signed in to iCloud, they sync across your own devices through Apple's iCloud (CloudKit) using your personal Apple ID and private iCloud container. This content stays within your Apple account; the developer has no access to it and cannot read it. Apple's handling of iCloud data is governed by Apple's Privacy Policy. You can turn iCloud sync off in your device settings at any time.

Network use

Scholar Phos works offline. All scripture, commentary, interlinear, and lexicon content is bundled with the app and read on-device. The only network activity is iCloud sync of your own content, handled entirely by Apple when you are signed in to iCloud. The app does not contact the developer or any third-party server.

Children

Scholar Phos is suitable for all ages and collects no personal data from anyone, including children.

Changes

If this policy changes, the “Last updated” date above will change and the app will note the update in its release notes.
